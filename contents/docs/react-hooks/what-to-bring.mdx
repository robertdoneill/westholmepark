## Bedding Items

Bring 1-2 sets of sheets, cozy blankets, a comforter, a mattress pad, pillows, and pillow cases. Comfortable bedding will really help your dorm room feel much more like home. Your mattress will be a twin XL (80” x 36”), so remember to bring bedding that fits!

Bedding items to bring:
- 1-2 sets of sheets (twin)
- Comforter (twin)
- Mattress pad (twin)
- Blankets
- Pillows
- Pillowcases
