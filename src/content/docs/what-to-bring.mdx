Your room at Westholme Park comes fully furnished, but there are a few things you'll want to bring to make it feel like home.

## Bedding

Your mattress is a **twin XL** (80" × 36"). Be sure to bring bedding that fits!

- 1–2 sets of sheets (twin XL)
- Comforter or duvet
- Mattress pad or topper
- Blankets
- Pillows & pillowcases

## Bathroom

- Towels (bath, hand, washcloth)
- Shower caddy
- Toiletries
- Bathrobe & slippers

## Kitchen & Dining

While the kitchen is fully equipped with appliances and cookware, you may want:

- Your own mug or water bottle
- Food storage containers
- Favorite snacks & spices

## Study & Electronics

- Laptop & charger
- Desk lamp (optional — rooms have overhead lighting)
- Headphones
- Power strip

## Personal Items

- Clothing for all seasons (LA is mild, but bring a light jacket)
- Laundry bag or hamper
- Hangers
- Personal decorations (photos, small plants, etc.)
- Reusable shopping bags

## What Not to Bring

- Large furniture (rooms are fully furnished)
- Candles or open-flame items
- Pets (sorry, no pets allowed)
- Weapons or hazardous materials
