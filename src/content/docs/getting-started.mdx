Welcome to Westholme Park! Here's what you need to know to get settled.

## Who We Serve

Westholme Park provides housing for female students attending UCLA, UCLA Extension, and Santa Monica College. Our community includes both graduate and undergraduate students, with private and shared bedroom options.

## Application Requirements

To reserve your spot, you'll need to provide:

- A valid passport
- A U.S. visa or school/work authorization
- Proof of funds

**Proof of funds** can be any of the following:
- Financial aid or student loans
- Pay stubs from U.S. employment
- College acceptance letter
- Bank statement showing sufficient funds for your stay

## How to Apply

1. **Submit your application** through our [online form](https://tally.so/r/mV86WJ) or email **join@westholmepark.com**.
2. **Schedule a tour** — virtual or in-person — to see the property and room options.
3. **Sign your lease** and submit your deposit to secure your room.
4. **Move in** on your scheduled date!
