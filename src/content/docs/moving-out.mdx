## Scheduling Your Move-Out

Move-out dates are your responsibility and must be completed online through the RentVine resident portal.

[Resident Portal Info](https://help.rentvine.com/resident-portal-introduction)

### Timing

- **Fixed-term leases:** Your move-out date should be set when you move in.
- **Month-to-month leases:** Give at least 30 days notice before your intended move-out date.
- **Unless your agreement states otherwise, move-outs are processed at the end of each month only.**

### How to Submit Notice

1. Log in to your RentVine resident portal.
2. Locate the box labeled **"Move-Out Notice."**
3. Fill in your move-out date, reason for leaving, and forwarding address.

## Security Deposit Refund

Your security deposit will be refunded within 21 days of your move-out date, minus any deductions for:

- Unpaid rent or fees
- Damage beyond normal wear and tear
- Cleaning costs if the room is not left in move-in condition

## Potential Charges

Common move-out charges include:

| Item | Typical Cost |
|------|-------------|
| Deep cleaning | $150–$300 |
| Carpet cleaning | $75–$150 |
| Wall repair / painting | $100–$400 |
| Missing keys / fobs | $25–$75 |
| Furniture damage | Varies |

> **Tip:** Do a thorough cleaning and document the room with photos before you leave to avoid disputes.
